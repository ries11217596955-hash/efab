# Governed Material Candidate

## Purpose
A found external source, file, repo, package, article or doc is not Builder's mind. It is a material candidate until reviewed.

## Classification
- `READ_REFERENCE_ONLY`
- `MATERIAL_CANDIDATE`
- `OFFICIAL_DOC_CANDIDATE`
- `EXECUTABLE_TOOL_CANDIDATE`
- `REJECTED_OR_QUARANTINED`

## Checks
1. What gap does this material solve?
2. Is it official or primary?
3. What is the version/platform/date?
4. What are license/terms?
5. Does it require install/execute?
6. What can be trusted and what must be verified?
7. What validator or smoke test proves usefulness?
8. What must not be copied or absorbed?

## Default flags
`trusted=false`, `accepted=false`, `install_allowed=false`, `execute_allowed=false`.

## Bridge to atoms
Only after the material closes a real gap and produces proof/use can Builder propose a bounded atom candidate.
